DILNI ABEYRATHNE ☰

KENTUKY'S SPPOKTACULAR PLACES

It was right around Halloween time when I first made this map. Foliage had started coloring and the air had a distinct sense of the unnatural, which made it the perfect atmosphere to make a spooky map tracking location of common spooks in Kentucky. Thus, this map was born.


Creating the Scariness


A map depicting spooky locations is not complete without a scary base design to complement it. This map created that atmosphere through a black-gray gradient Kentucky base layer coupled with river line data in crimson ("rivers of blood") and roads in black. With a little Illustrator magic, this base gives off cursed heart vibes, which is exactly the theme I needed. To add to the horror is the ghostly figure on the left and some interesting tidbits about two of the most haunted and abandoned places in Kentucky as noted by inidivual accounts. This map was meant to be more of a fun endeavor, so I took certain liberties with its design and went completely Halloween mode.
